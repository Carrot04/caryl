Natural Sciences Library

> VISION

Our vision is to be one the leading science library in the Philippines: Envisioning a future where
scientific knowledge can manifest a positive impact.
Our library will be a vibrant hub of intellectual curiosity, collaboration, and connecting the patron in the world and spectrum of natural science.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                

> MISSION

The Eunoia Library’s mission is to provide a broad access of information resources and services to patrons,
providing inclusive and profound resources and services. Our library will foster scientific literacy, critical thinking
and creativity for diverse learners and researchers.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                

> GOALS

To provide a wide range of library and research services in supporting the formal and informal education setting.
To provide excellent instructions and services that ensures extensive collaboration, knowledge sharing,
and engagement for colleges, universities, and the local community up to the whole nation.
Additionally, to preserve and maintain valuable scientific resources, guaranteeing that they remain available for future generations.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                

> OBJECTIVES

The following are objectives that will guide our efforts and ensure the alignment of our actions with the desired outcomes:

  • To provide scholarly resources in several formats, aiding facilities and user-centered functions of the community.
  • To serve quality information services and resources in meeting their needs.
  • To formulate up-to-date collections, systematic, timely, relevant selective acquisition of print and non-print reference materials of suitable quality and format.
  • Enhance user experience through inclusive collection and digital resources.
  • Promote literacy, critical thinking, and creativity.
  • Support research excellence and innovations.
